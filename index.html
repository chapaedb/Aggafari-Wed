Your premier wedding planning service in Ethiopia!

Our team of experienced planners is dedicated to making your special day unforgettable. From the moment you contact us, we work tirelessly to understand your vision for your wedding day and bring it to life. What We Do

Weddings and Event Planning

At Aggafari-Wed, we offer a range of wedding planning services to ensure that your special day is stress-free and unforgettable. Our services include: Venue selection: We'll help you find the perfect wedding venue that fits your style and budget, whether it's an outdoor garden or an elegant ballroom.
Catering: We work with top catering companies to provide delicious food and drinks that will delight your guests.
Floral arrangements: Our expert florists will create stunning floral arrangements that complement your wedding theme and style.
Music and entertainment: We'll help you find the perfect music and entertainment for your wedding, whether it's a live band or a DJ.
Photography and videography: We work with talented photographers and videographers to capture every special moment of your wedding day. Transportation: We can arrange transportation for you and your guests, whether it's a classic car or a luxury limousine.
