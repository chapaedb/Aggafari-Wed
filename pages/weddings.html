What We offer

Styling & Event Decoration

Bridal and Bridesmaid Bouquet

At Aggafari Wedding Organizer, we understand the importance of flowers in creating a beautiful and memorable wedding. That's why we offer a range of stunning bridal and bridesmaid bouquets that are customized to suit your style and preferences.

Our team of experienced florists will work with you to create the perfect bouquet that complements your wedding theme and color scheme. We use only the freshest and highest quality flowers to ensure that your bouquet looks and smells amazing on your special day

Corsages and Boutonnieres

Our team of experienced florists will work with you to create the perfect corsages and boutonnieres that complement your wedding theme and color scheme. We use only the freshest and highest quality flowers to ensure that your corsages and boutonnieres look and smell amazing on your special day.
Whether you prefer a classic and elegant design or something more modern and unique, we have the expertise to create something truly special. We also offer a range of options for corsages and boutonnieres, including matching or complementary designs to the bridal and bridesmaid bouquets.

Head Dresses

We understand the importance of accessories in creating the perfect look for your special day. One accessory that can add a unique touch to your wedding attire is a head dress. We offer a variety of head dress options to complement your wedding theme and style.
From floral headbands to elegant tiaras, our head dresses are made with high-quality materials and are designed to enhance your beauty on your special day. We can also customize head dresses to match your wedding colors and personal taste.

Reception and Ceremony Decoration

we understand that the decoration is an essential element of your wedding ceremony and reception. We offer a wide range of decoration options to create a beautiful and unforgettable atmosphere for your special day.
Our team of experienced decorators will work with you to understand your vision and bring it to life. We can create custom decorations that match your wedding colors, theme, and personal taste.
