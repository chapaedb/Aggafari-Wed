Who are we

At Aggafari Wedding Organizer, we understand that your wedding day is one of the most important days of your life. That's why we go above and beyond to ensure that every detail is perfect. From the venue selection and decor to the catering and entertainment, we take care of everything, so you don't have to worry about a thing.
Our team is made up of highly skilled and experienced professionals who are passionate about creating personalized and unforgettable experiences for our clients. We also offer a range of wedding planning services, including full-service planning, partial planning, and day-of coordination. Whether you're looking for a grand and extravagant celebration or a small and intimate gathering, we will work with you to bring your vision to life.
At Aggafari Wedding Organizer, we pride ourselves on our attention to detail, creativity, and exceptional customer service. Our goal is to exceed your expectations and create a wedding that is truly unforgettable.
Contact us today to schedule a consultation and let us help you plan the wedding of your dreams.
